Accommodation


As you can imagine, Boston and the area offer a huge variety of options – from simple motels outside the city to luxury hotels in the city center. Below, we just list a few options in different areas. Of course, there are many more and depending on your travel plans (e.g., whether you are staying longer in Boston, have a (rental) car,…) one option might work better for you than another.

The travel times to and from the ceremony/reception locations mentioned below are all estimates and will depend on actual traffic conditions!


Accommodations near the reception location (Bellevue Golf Club)

These places might be best if you are traveling by (or renting a) car, especially if you are planning an extended stay in the greater North Shore area.

Holiday Inn Express Saugus (Logan Airport), an IHG Hotel

8-minute drive from Bellevue Golf Club, ~ 20-minute drive to Faith Lutheran Church (shortest route includes a short toll route)

Address: 999 Broadway, Saugus, MA 01906

Red Roof PLUS+ Boston – Logan

6-minute drive from Bellevue Golf Club, ~ 25-minute drive to Faith Lutheran Church (shortest route includes a short toll route)

Address: 920 Broadway, Saugus, MA

Econo Lodge Boston-Malden

8-minute drive from Bellevue Golf Club, ~ 20-minute drive to Faith Lutheran Church (shortest route includes a short toll route)

Address: 321 Broadway, Malden, MA 02148

Accommodations near the ceremony location (Faith Lutheran Church)

The hotels below are located in Cambridge, from where both Cambridge and Boston can easily be explored by public transportation, renting a Blue Bike, or walking. After the reception, it would be best to get (back) to those hotels by car/Uber/Lyft/…

Hotel Veritas

16-minute walk (or 4-minute drive) to Faith Lutheran Church, ~ 30-minute drive from Bellevue Golf Club

Address: 1 Remington Street, Cambridge, MA 02138

Irving House at Harvard

16-minute walk (or 4-minute drive) to Faith Lutheran Church, ~ 30-minute drive from Bellevue Golf Club

Excellent breakfast (we have tried it)!

Address: 24 Irving Street, Cambridge, MA 02138

Porter Square Hotel

8-minute drive (or 12-minute bus ride) to Faith Lutheran Church, ~ 25-minute drive from Bellevue Golf Club

Located right next to a Red Line stop (Porter Square)

Address: 1924 Massachusetts Ave., Cambridge, MA 02140

Le Meridien Boston Cambridge

13-minute walk (or 5-minute drive) to Faith Lutheran Church, ~ 23-minute drive from Bellevue Golf Club

Address: 20 Sidney St, Cambridge, MA 02139

Accommodations in Downtown Boston

The hotels below are located in Downtown Boston, from where the city can easily be explored during an extended stay by public transportation, renting a Blue Bike, or walking. For getting to the ceremony location and back from the reception, driving (or Uber, Lyft…) might be the best option. Of course, there is a plethora of other options available in Boston, we just picked three examples!

Battery Wharf Hotel

15-minute drive to ceremony location, ~ 20-minute drive from Bellevue Golf Club (shortest route includes a short toll route)

CitizenM Boston North Station Hotel

Omni Parker House
